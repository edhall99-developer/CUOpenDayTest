 topics and then has its own internal scroll so users can still have a filtering system even with the limited space of tablet and mobile

event as the ui is getting very overwhelmed by the large number of events. I have 
split this out to only shoow the next event occurence and then a 'show all' button

micromodal https://www.npmjs.com/package/micromodal?activeTab=readme)
